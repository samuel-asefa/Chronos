<h2 align="center"><em><code>Chronos</code></em></h2>

A history trivia game where you place events on the timeline.

## Features
- Multiple choice questions with a 20-second countdown
- Year slider questions where points scale with how close you are
- Streak multiplier when you get 3 correct answers in a row
- Three lives system
- Full question review at the end

## How to Play 
Pick a category, get 10 random questions, and try to survive all 3 lives. Multiple choice gives you a time bonus for fast answers. Slider questions reward accuracy — within 5 years is correct, within 20 is partial credit (but costs a life).
